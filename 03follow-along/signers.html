Who Were the Signers

Famous Signers

Some of the most famous signers of the Declaration of Independence include

Thomas Jefferson
Principal author of the Declaration of Independence and later the third U.S. president.
John Adams
A key advocate for independence and later the second U.S. president.
Benjamin Franklin
A renowned inventor, diplomat, and one of the most influential Founding Fathers.
John Hancock
President of the Continental Congress, known for his large, bold signature.
Samuel Adams
A major leader in the push for American independence and organizer of the Boston Tea Party.
Richard Henry Lee
Introduced the resolution for independence to the Continental Congress.
Roger Sherman
The only person to sign all four major founding documents of the U.S.

The Declaration of Independence was signed by 56 men, but some stood out as particularly influential in shaping the future of the United States. Together, these leaders played a crucial role in securing American freedom and laying the foundation for a new nation.
